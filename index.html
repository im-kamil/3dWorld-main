Welcome to 3dWorld: Explore History in 3D


At 3dWorld, we bring the world's historical treasures to your fingertips through meticulously crafted 3D models of old and historical architectures. Our mission is twofold: to offer a stunningly immersive experience to those unable to visit these sites in person, and to alleviate the environmental impact caused by mass tourism on these delicate locations.


Why 3dWorld?


1- Exploration: Step into the past and explore iconic architectural marvels from around the globe in lifelike 3D detail.

2-Preservation Efforts: By reducing physical foot traffic, we contribute to the preservation and longevity of these cultural landmarks.

3-Educational Value: Gain a deeper understanding of history and architecture through interactive virtual tours and educational resources.


Join Us


Whether you're an enthusiast seeking historical knowledge or an educator enhancing classroom learning, 3dWorld invites you to discover, learn, and appreciate our shared heritage from the comfort of your screen.
